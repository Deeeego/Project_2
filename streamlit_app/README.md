MLB 2021 Odds
This streamlit app will try and answer different questions about the MLB using the betting odds.

Questions
How often do the favorites cover their run lines? 
Is there any correlation between higher/lower totals based on the stadium played at? 
What is the average line for home vs. away teams?
Are there seasonal patterns?
Do certain months consistently show higher totals?

Dataset Information:
mlb-odds-2021
https://sports-statistics.com/sports-data/mlb-historical-odds-scores-datasets/
4924 rows
Visitor and Home are right next to each other on the dataset. You combine those together to get a
complete game. Dates are also in the form of 3 numbers. First char is the month and the last 2 chars
are the day of the game played.
